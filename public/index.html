Is DoneGrading allowed under FERPA and COPPA?
Yes. DoneGrading is built with FERPA/COPPA in mind. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and is processed only to deliver the features you use. We never sell student data or use it to train public models.
Does it work with Google Classroom?
Absolutely. Sign in with Google, sync your Classroom courses, and post grades and feedback directly. No double‑entry, no fragile CSV imports.
Will this replace my judgment?
No. DoneGrading suggests scores and feedback based on your rubric, but you review, adjust, and approve every decision. You stay the professional in the loop.
What if I don't actually save time or don’t like it?
You get a 7‑day free trial. If DoneGrading doesn’t save you at least 10 minutes a day, cancel before the trial ends — you pay nothing. After that it’s 67¢/day and you can cancel anytime.
Does it work on my phone?
Yes. DoneGrading runs on iOS, Android, and web. Scan papers with your phone, finish feedback on your laptop, or do both in between periods.
